I am an assistant professor in the department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CSE@CUHK), where I work on automated techniques for formally reasoning about programs. I am interested in (a) applying these techniques to help people understand and correctly interact with existing software components and (b) figuring out how to scale these techniques so that they can be usefully applied to real systems. More specific recent interests are in applications of static analysis and synthesis to LLM interpretability and automated reasoning about concurrent and distributed systems.
